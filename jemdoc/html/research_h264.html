Research

Full hardware H.264/AVC encoder

I am a core member of the project from 2011 to 2014. We developed a full hardware H.264/AVC encoder. My major task is to develop the inter-prediction module. Selected publications:

  1. Ngoc-Mai Nguyen, Edith Beigne, Suzanne Lesecq, Duy-Hieu Bui, Nam-Khanh Dang, Xuan-Tu Tran, “H.264AVC Hardware Encoders and Low-Power Features”/, 2014 IEEE Asia Pacific Conference on Circuits & Systems (APCCAS 2014), Nov. 17-20, 2014.

  2. Nam-Khanh Dang, Xuan-Tu Tran, Alain Merigot, “An Efficient Hardware Architecture for Inter-Prediction in H.264AVC Encoders”/,17th IEEE Symposium on Design and Diagnostics of Electronic Circuits and Systems (DDECS 2014), Apr. 23-25, 2014.
